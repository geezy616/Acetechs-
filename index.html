About Acetechs (chrispine744@gmail.com)

Acetechs (chrispine744@gmail.com) is a non-profit organization focused on unlocking Africa's untapped potential by providing solar energy and internet access. We challenge monopolies and work directly with rural communities to transform children's futures through power and connectivity.

The Problems We Tackle

Many African countries face extreme monopoly in power transmission and network access. Electricity remains unaffordable or unavailable for millions, with schools, clinics, and homes operating in the dark. Access to digital tools is minimal, cutting off educational opportunities.

Our Solutions

We deploy decentralized solar grid systems and partner with organizations rolling out wireless internet access in rural communities. Our projects bring light, knowledge, and opportunity directly into the hands of children and families.
